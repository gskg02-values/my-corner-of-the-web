Did You Know?

Honey Never Spoils

Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old and are still perfectly edible.

Octopus Hearts

An octopus has three hearts. Two pump blood to the gills, while the third circulates it to the rest of the body.

Space Smell

According to astronauts, space smells like a combination of seared steak, hot metal, and welding fumes.
